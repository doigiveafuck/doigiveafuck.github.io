- In short -

It's probably gonna be the most useful app you will ever encountered - and with a high probability it will at least once save your ass. Available for all modern iOS and Android devices.

- In detail -

Most of us struggle throughout our lives by giving too many fucks in situations where fucks do not deserve to be given. Have you ever wondered: what if we all have a limited number of fucks to give? How many times have you asked yourself:To give a fuck, or not to give a fuck?

Three exciting animations are waiting to give you the answer in funny and interesting way. Delivered to you in form of border-breaking and earth-shattering mobile app. We don't intend to earn our first million on it - it will be available for free in Google Play and Apple Store.
